## The problem

Brilliant technology does not sell itself. We hear the same conversations every week:

> *"We raised €8M, we have a working chip, and no idea how to sell it."*

> *"Our investors are asking about pipeline and we don't even have a CRM."*

> *"We hired a sales guy from a large semiconductor company. Six months in, nothing closed. He is used to inheriting a $200M installed base, not building from zero."*

The pattern is the same every time. The R&D worked. The funding came through. But nobody in the building has ever priced a semiconductor, structured a distribution agreement, or closed a production contract with a Tier 1 customer.

Every month without a commercial function is runway burned with no pipeline to show for it. Competitors are locking in design wins. The window between *funded* and *too late* is shorter than most founders think.

---

## What we do

We embed with your team and operate as your commercial function from Day 1.

* **Market and product intelligence.** Which customers need your product, at what price point, against which competitors.
* **Go-to-market strategy.** Which markets to enter, in what sequence, through what channels.
* **Selling.** Customer meetings, pricing discussions, contract negotiation, deal closing. We represent you in front of your customers as if we were your own team. For the duration of the engagement, we are.
* **Pipeline and revenue generation.** Target accounts, customer introductions, design-in tracking, CRM setup.
* **Commercial infrastructure.** Pricing strategy, sales process, distribution setup, and contracts through to signed production agreements.
* **Team build-out.** When you are ready, we recruit, onboard, and coach your permanent commercial team and hand over a running operation.

## Why us

**We execute.** We sit in the customer meeting with you. We build your CRM. We draft the contract. We close the deal. Then we build the team that does it without us.

---

## How we work

**Phase 1: Market Entry Sprint (first 90 days)**
Market study, competitive landscape, pricing benchmarks, customer segmentation, target account list, first customer introductions. You get a board-ready market analysis, a go-to-market playbook, and a live pipeline.

**Phase 2: Embedded Commercial Leadership (ongoing)**
We operate as your commercial team two to three days per week. Pipeline management, customer meetings, contract negotiation, CRM management, reporting to your CEO and board.

**Phase 3: Commercial Build-Out (when you are ready)**
We hire your first sales lead, define the compensation structure, set the KPIs, onboard the team, and transition the operation. Then we step back.

Not every company needs all three phases. Some need a 90-day sprint to validate a market. Others need embedded commercial leadership for two years. We scope and price every engagement individually.

---

## What we are not

**Not a consulting firm.** Consulting firms deliver a slide deck, shake hands, and wish you luck with execution. We stay for the execution.

**Not a sales rep.** Reps carry your bag and take a commission. We do market intelligence, deal execution, and build the permanent organization that replaces us.

**Not a recruiter.** Recruiters find you a VP Sales candidate and collect a fee. Whether that person succeeds is your problem. We prove the commercial model first, then hire the right person into a machine that already works.
