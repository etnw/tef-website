Etienne Winkelmuller
Founder — Munich

Etienne has 25+ years in semiconductors, building product lines from zero to revenue, leading post-M&A integration, and negotiating production contracts with Tier 1 and OEM customers across Europe, the US, and Asia.

At IDT/Renesas, he created the company's first automotive-qualified timing portfolio, growing it from zero to over $10M in revenue and driving the organizational transformation to AEC-Q100 and IATF 16949 compliance. At indie Semiconductor, he took full P&L ownership of the $10M radar product line, launched a 120 GHz in-cabin radar with a $100M+ opportunity funnel, and converted a low-volume specialty manufacturer into a high-volume automotive supplier, cutting wafer cost by 70% through foundry migration.

He holds an MBA from the Isenberg School of Management and a M.Sc. in Physics, Optics, and Electronics from ENSPS Strasbourg. ISO 26262 certified. Trilingual: English, German, French. He is also a licensed pilot and aircraft owner.
